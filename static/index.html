RPG Buddy for Wave

Introduction

RPG Buddy for Wave can be used for playing dice-based role playing games in Google Wave. To use, add rpgbuddy@appspot.com to your wave. Once added, it will replace strings of the following formats with dice rolls when enclosed in double square brackets:

Roll String Example Output Explanation
[[4d6]] [ 3 6 1 1 ] = 11 Roll four, six-sided dice.
[[3d6o6]] [ 5 1 6 5 ] = 12 Roll three, six-sided dice, the final die should be rerolled (remain open) if a six or greater is rolled.
[[1d20+2]] [ 20 + 2 ] = 22 Roll one, twenty-sided die and add two to the result.
[[5d6o6+1]] [ 4 4 5 6 1 + 1 ] = 21 Roll five, six-sided dice, remain open if a six or greater is rolled last, then add one to the result.

Notice that the final die rolled is highlighted, in case is holds significance, and the result is made bold for convenience.
